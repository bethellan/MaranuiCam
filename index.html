About This View

This live webcam looks out over Lyall Bay from the iconic Maranui Surf Life Saving Club in Wellington, New Zealand.

Lyall Bay is a popular spot for surfers, swimmers, and walkers. The Maranui SLSC is a historic club and also home to the famous Maranui Café.
